About

Ambar B Capoor is a Cinematographer based in Hollywood. Ambar enjoys working on projects that are collaborative, creative and compelling. His background as an actor and producer allow him to approach projects with a unique perspective and produce truly exceptional pieces. He is comfortable working on projects in any country, environment and budget. He has worked on multicam projects with Elton John, Taylor Swift and AC/DC as well as single cam projects with Lady Gaga and Juhi Chawla.

Clients include Dr. Dre, Lady Gaga, Samsung, Bank of Hope, and Hyundai.
